who we are

an independent delegation built for norcal.

omni mun is a student-run independent delegation based in northern california. we send delegates to top-tier model un conferences up and down the state, competing against the best delegations in the country.

whether you're a first-time delegate or a seasoned chair, omni mun gives you the structure, coaching, and community to perform your best on the floor.

the process

how omni mun works

step one

register & join the delegation

sign up on this site and fill out the conference form. once registration closes, you'll get added to the omni mun group chat where we coordinate everything — payments, hotel rooms, and prep.

register for smunc xxx
step two

prepare with the team

we do group prep before every conference — position papers, committee research, and strategy sessions. whether you're a veteran or it's your first conference, omni mun coaches you to walk in ready.

step three

compete & represent

show up to the conference as part of one of norcal's most competitive independent delegations. debate on the floor, build your network, and leave with skills you'll actually use — win or not.
