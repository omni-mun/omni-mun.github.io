Who We Are

An independent delegation built for NorCal delegates.

Omni MUN is a student-run independent delegation based in Northern California. We send delegates to top-tier Model UN conferences up and down the state, competing against the best delegations in the country.

Whether you're a first-time delegate or a seasoned chair, Omni MUN gives you the structure, coaching, and community to perform your best on the floor.
